Our Process

Streamlined Reporting Workflow

A proven 5-step process designed for speed, accuracy, and seamless integration with your facility.

  1. 1

    Scan Upload

    Scans completed and uploaded to the cloud. Your facility uploads the imaging study securely to our cloud-based PACS system.

  2. 2

    Radiologist Review

    A subspecialty-trained radiologist is immediately assigned based on modality and study type for expert interpretation.

  3. 3

    Report Optimization

    Reports optimized in prescribed formats. Structured according to your facility's templates and requirements.

  4. 4

    Quality Control

    Final quality control by senior radiologist. Comprehensive review ensuring accuracy, completeness, and clinical relevance.

  5. 5

    Report Delivery

    Final report submitted within agreed turnaround time. Delivered back to your system meeting all SLA commitments.
